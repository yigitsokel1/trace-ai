# Billing FAQ

## Invoices and receipts

Invoices are emailed on the billing date and available under **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. PDF receipts include tax IDs when provided at checkout.

## Payment methods

We accept major credit cards and PayPal. Failed payments retry up to three times over 72 hours before the subscription enters a grace period.

## Duplicate charges

If you see two charges for the same period, contact support with both transaction IDs. We reconcile against the payment processor within one business day.
