# Refund Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 14 days of purchase for annual plans and within 7 days for monthly plans.

## Eligibility

- Duplicate charges are refunded automatically after verification.
- Partial refunds apply when usage exceeds the fair-use threshold documented in billing.
- Chargebacks initiated through the bank may delay refund processing by 5–10 business days.

## How to request

Reply with the account email, invoice ID, and reason. Our team confirms eligibility against billing records before issuing credit.
